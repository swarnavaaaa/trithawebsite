The Alignment

Why work with me?

Because therapy should feel like a conversation, not an interrogation.

With me, you don't need to have perfect answers.

You don't need to know exactly what's wrong.

You don't need to worry about saying the right thing.

You can show up confused, emotional, angry, scared, numb, or uncertain.

We'll work through it together.

Clients often tell me that they appreciate having a space where they feel genuinely listened to, understood, and accepted.

What therapy with me is like

  • Warm and supportive
  • Honest and non-judgmental
  • Focused on understanding rather than blaming
  • A space where difficult emotions are welcome
  • Collaborative rather than directive
  • Grounded in your pace and your goals

There is no pressure to share more than you're ready for. We go one step at a time.

What therapy with me is not

  • Me giving advice every session
  • Me telling you what decisions to make
  • Being judged for your choices
  • Being expected to 'stay positive'
  • Being pushed before you're ready

I believe people grow best when they feel safe, understood, and accepted.

Session Structure

Online & in-person

I offer both online and in-person therapy sessions. Online sessions provide flexibility and convenience, while in-person sessions offer a dedicated space to talk face-to-face.

Together, we can decide what works best for you and your lifestyle.
